❓ Detailed FAQ & Troubleshooting

Why is my player falling through the floor on room load?

Ensure the PlayerSpawnPoint is at least 0.75 units above the floor collider.

The Lobby environment is still visible while in a room.

Verify your Lobby is childed to a single Root GameObject assigned to the Main Scene Root.

Can I trigger a "Death" or "Game Over" state?

Yes. Call LoadRespawnPoint() from your health or damage script to return to the lobby.
